isEmailValid(email) → {boolean}

Validates an email address according to custom rules. The rules are: 1. Email must have just one '@' character. 2. '@' splits 'email' into 'name part' and 'domain parts'. 3. Name part must have at least 8 characters. 4. Name part must contain at least 6 letters. 5. Name part must contain no more than 2 numbers. 6. '.' splits domain parts into provider and domain. 7. Provider must be valid and from the list. 8. Domain must contain just letters and at least 2 of them.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
email string The email address to validate.

Returns:
'true' if valid, 'false' otherwise.
