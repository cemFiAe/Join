isEmailValid(email) → {boolean}

Validates an email address according to custom rules: - Must have exactly one "@" symbol. - The name part (before "@") must be at least 8 characters long. - The name must contain at least 6 letters. - The name must contain no more than 2 numbers. - The domain must have exactly one dot, with a valid provider and valid domain extension.
Parameters:
Name Type Description
email string The email address to validate.
